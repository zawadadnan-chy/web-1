DESCRIPTION

There are 7 types of hybridisation of Atomic orbitals, these are sp,sp2,sp3,sp3d,sp3d2,sp3d3. hybridisation is basically the mixing of atomic orbitals of similar or nearly similar energy. The molecule which is hybridised and have more s characters in hybrid orbitals are more acidic in nature because they tends to attracts their orbital electrons as s orbitals are more closer to neucleus then other orbitals. We know that according to Lewis theory the compound that attracts the lone pair electrons are acidic in nature.
